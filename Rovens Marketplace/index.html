App Description

An instrument online shop app, where people can sell their instruments and buyers choose their preferences on what they want to buy

The Problem

This app makes both the sellers and buyers life easier, for example people who want to buy something from their country and they dont want to pay the extra shipping costs etc

There arent alot of online instrument shops in Estonia

Customer Segment

The app is intended for everyone who want to for example sell their instruments, but dont have the money to sell on an actual company
